I am a graduate student at the National University of Singapore pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) jointly taught by the NUS Business School and NUS School of Computing. I'm passionate about using data to tackle complex challenges in renewable energy, green finance, and financial technology. In 2024, I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence, India with distinction.

Prior to attending graduate school, I was an Analyst Intern at Bain & Company in the Performance Improvement Practice in New Delhi. I specialized in Data & Tech and focused on advancing Operations Excellence benchmarking capabilities and procurement-related cost transformations (Buy Better + Spend Better®).

Previously, I interned at Paisabazaar.com, India's largest and most trusted platform for financial products, developing prediction models for credit card limits and disbursals. I also interned at TransOrg Analytics and worked part-time at Spenny (now part of CRED) , a Y-combinator backed micro-savings app. Before university, I interned at ITC Hotels, focusing on sustainable engineering practices.

I'm always happy to mentor young professionals starting out their academic or professional journeys. If you'd like to collaborate, drop me an email! Why do I do it? Because people gave me a shot and it changed my trajectory. I am paying it forward.

I am best reached by email at shlok.kamat@comp.nus.edu.sg. Feel free to reach out about my research or anything else I might be able to help with. For more details, check my CV
