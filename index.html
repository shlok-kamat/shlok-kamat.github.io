I am a final-year Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) candidate at the National University of Singapore (NUS), specializing in healthcare analytics and consumer technology applications. I have been priviledged to receive interdisciplinary training from NUS Business School and NUS Computing allowing me to develop targeted expertise in these high-impact sectors.

Currently, I serve as a Data Analyst Intern at Arta Finance, where I contribute to data-driven decision-making initiatives that directly impact the company's strategic growth and notable recent publicity. This role has deepened my practical understanding of financial analytics and technology implementation within the fintech industry.

Prior to my graduate studies, I gained valuable experience as an Analyst in Bain & Company's New Delhi office where I specialized in data & technology within the performance improvement practice. My work focused on enhancing operations excellence benchmarking capabilities and leading procurement-related cost transformation projects, including Bain’s proprietary Buy Better + Spend Better® framework.

Previously, I interned at Paisabazaar.com, India's largest and most trusted platform for financial products, developing prediction models for credit card limits and disbursals. I also interned at TransOrg Analytics and worked part-time at Spenny (now part of CRED), a Y-combinator backed micro-savings app.
