About Me

Hi, I’m Shlok - a final-year Master of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) candidate at the National University of Singapore (NUS), where I specialize in financial risk analytics, healthcare analytics, and consumer tech applications.

Beyond the classroom, I was a Finalist at the NUS-GURU Network AI Innovation Challenge 2025, and an AFP® professional in Singapore.

In April 2025, I will be joining EY Singapore as a technology consultant in the AI & Data team.

Most recently, I was a data analyst intern at Arta Finance, where I automated AUM tracking (saving advisory teams 15+ hours weekly), built a semantic data layer to simplify analytics, and co-developed a platform powering Arta’s AI suite for it's “Wealth as a service” offering.

Before grad school, I was an Analyst at Bain & Company in New Delhi, working within the performance improvement practice. I supported operations excellence and cost transformation projects, including Bain’s proprietary Buy Better + Spend Better® framework.

I also interned at Paisabazaar.com, developing prediction models for credit card limits and disbursals. Prior roles include internships at TransOrg Analytics and Spenny (now part of CRED), a Y-combinator backed micro-savings app.

I graduated with distinction in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Shiv Nadar University, India, where I also led national-level design competitions and took a strong interest in finance electives.

I am best reached by email at shlok.kamat@comp.nus.edu.sg. Feel free to reach out about my university experience, internships, or anything else I might be able to help with. For more details, check my CV
